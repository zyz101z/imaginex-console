HOW TO PLAY

Destroy the other tank! Shells bounce off walls — and yes, your own shell can come back and get you. First to 5 rounds wins the match.

Player 1 (amber): WASD press where you want to go (combine keys for diagonals)  •  SPACE or F fire

Player 2 (teal): ↑↓←→ press where you want to go  •  ENTER or M fire

Your tank shoots the way it's facing — tap a direction to aim.

Touch (solo): left side of the screen steers, tap the right side to fire.

Power-ups: ▲ TRIPLE 3-way spread  •  ⚡ RAPID fast fire  •  ⬤ SHIELD blocks one hit  •  ■ BIG SHOT smashes through walls
