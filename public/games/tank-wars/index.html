HOW TO PLAY

Destroy the other tank! Shells bounce off walls — and yes, your own shell can come back and get you. First to 5 rounds wins the match.

Drive (amber tank): WASD or ↑↓←→ — press where you want to go (combine keys for diagonals)

Fire: SPACE F ENTER or M

Your tank shoots the way it's facing — tap a direction to aim.

Touch: press the left side — a joystick appears under your thumb (tilt more to drive faster). Hold the FIRE button on the right to shoot.

Power-ups: ▲ TRIPLE 3-way spread  •  ⚡ RAPID fast fire  •  ⬤ SHIELD blocks one hit  •  ■ BIG SHOT smashes through walls

Camp too long and SUDDEN DEATH starts crumbling the maze...

Earn SCRAP every round (+10 win, +2 otherwise, big match bonuses) and spend it in the GARAGE on tanks with totally different weapons. Climb the 10-battle CAMPAIGN for stars and the biggest rewards.
